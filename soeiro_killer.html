Killer Name :

El Melocotón

Skills :

Usually El Melocotón strangles people to death, and after it he collects their organs.

About :

Creeping from the city's shadows, El Melocotón, rooted in Romanian tales, casts an unsettling aura. Strangulation sets the stage, a macabre introduction to a bone-chilling performance: the calculated removal of organs. A city shudders, realizing a malevolent character from Romanian lore roams freely. Time races as law enforcement scrambles to unveil El Melocotón's identity, lest more lives fall victim to this haunting tale.
